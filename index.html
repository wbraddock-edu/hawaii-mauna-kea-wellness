A Sanctuary Rooted in Aloha

Nestled in the cool, misty highlands of Waimea, our spa was founded on the belief that true healing honors the whole person — body, mind, and spirit.

Every treatment draws on the wisdom of traditional Hawaiian healing arts passed down through generations. Our therapists are trained in authentic lomi lomi techniques alongside modern therapeutic practices.

The volcanic energy of Mauna Kea infuses every session, from the basalt stones we use in our hot stone treatments to the native botanical ingredients in our skincare rituals.
