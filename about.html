Our Story

Rooted in the healing wisdom of Hawaii, dedicated to your restoration.

Born from the Land, Guided by Aloha

Mauna Kea Wellness Spa was born from a simple, profound vision: to create a place where the ancient healing wisdom of Hawaii could be shared with anyone seeking restoration of body, mind, and spirit.

Founded in 2012 by Leilani Kahananui, a fifth-generation practitioner of traditional Hawaiian healing arts, the spa was established in the misty highlands of Waimea — where the volcanic energy of Mauna Kea meets the gentle rains and cool mountain air that make this place so uniquely healing.

Leilani trained for over a decade under Hawaiian healing masters before opening our doors, and every practice in the spa — from the botanicals we use to the intentions we set — honors that lineage.

The Waimea Setting

Our spa is nestled at 2,670 feet elevation in the paniolo (cowboy) town of Waimea — one of Hawaii Island's best-kept secrets. The cool mountain air, sweeping views of Mauna Kea, and lush green pastures create a naturally therapeutic environment.

Unlike beach spas, our highland setting brings stillness, mist, and the unique volcanic energy that flows down from the mountain itself. Many guests tell us they feel the healing begin the moment they step out of their car.

We are located on Mamalahoa Highway with easy parking, just minutes from Waimea town center and a scenic 45-minute drive from Kailua-Kona.
